Privacy Policy for JumpCut

Last Updated: 11/20/2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to JumpCut. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, protect, and disclose information obtained from users of the JumpCut Chrome extension.

2. Information Collection and Use

JumpCut does not collect any personal information from its users. The extension operates entirely within the user's browser and does not transmit any data to external servers.

3. Data Storage and Protection

As JumpCut does not collect personal data, there is no storage or transfer of such data. All processing is done locally on the user's device.

4. User Consent

By using JumpCut, users consent to this privacy policy.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
